All YOU CAN EAT

Posted by Yusuf Shaikhnag

Promos, Weekends, Breakfast

Right next to the Gate way of India is Bade miya. Crowded all the time. Hard to find parking. Eat on / in ur car or sit in the small place they've put tables and chairs is ur choice. People go there for the food not the ambience or anythg else. The veg and non veg are cooked seperate. Even the plates for vegs and non vegs are marked seperate. Imp Tip: Order all ur food in one go or u may have to wait longer. Vegs have fewer options. The Veg sheek kababs, paneer tikka and daal tadka with rumali roti was my order and was yummy. Veg biryani was ok. But must visit this place atleast once as it is one of Mumbai's oldest.

Jan 31st

Eating Day!

Posted by Ubedullah

Promos, Weekends, Breakfast

Been here a couple of times and have tried a few dishes. First of all, the place is quite hyped and unnecessarily blown out of proportion. The food is not all that great and neither is the seating arrangement. I have had mutton roll, chicken roll - both very average and mumbai needs some good rolls joint. Also binged upon their chicken curry, mutton rogan and keema kaleji - again very average and not much impressive.
