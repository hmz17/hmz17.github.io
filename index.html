Welcome to Bade Miyan!

This place is one of Mumbai's legendary joints which every true foodie has at some time visited at midnight and eaten the rolls on the bonnet of your or someone else's car.

The place has now fallen in estimation not because of fall in quality, the baida roti, chicken tikka & boti kabab rolls, kaleji (almost always out of stock) and chicken bhuna still taste as good, but the available options have widened dramatically. While the kababs / rolls here were among the best in Bombay earlier they don't stand up as well in comparison to say a kakori kabab from kakori house or other newer places..

Also the seating in the abandoned building next door is quite shady, have seen rats there twice. Also the newer items on the menu don't seem to match up to the traditional favourites. The owners seem to have realized this and have opened another posher outlet, Bademiya Fine Dine, near Fort with better ambience for family crowds.

Nevertheless Bademiya remains a good option for a quick bite preferably late in the night and I guess you can't really call yourself a foodie in Mumbai unless you have been here sometime...
